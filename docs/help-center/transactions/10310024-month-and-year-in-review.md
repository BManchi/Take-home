# Month and Year in Review

**Source:** https://help.copilot.money/en/articles/10310024-month-and-year-in-review

Copilot's Month and Year in Reviews give you a simple, high level report of your spending, income, investments and related trends for the given time frame. You can share any slide to a contact or socially by tapping at the bottom of the report.
​
The **Month in Review** can be found in the Transactions tab at the end /start of each calendar month.

Additionally, the **Year in Review**can be found at the end/ start of the year.
[![screenshot](../../screenshots/transactions/02-image.png)](https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/gw2wbwl7/1306561727/1942fe31cf34517a9aae52b2628d/image.png?expires=1773322200&signature=936bed74c3c044403b5994fd2aeae3294a12208557d935e8160cdaacb06cc15e&req=dSMnEMx4nIZdXvMW1HO4zbNzj%2BZXHINMA2fU1OjYsFuP%2BjbZJVKNtPEmIm67%0AwXm8JPCRTceJrZiRTWc%3D%0A)
After being with Copilot for more than one year, you'll receive a special app icon in the following next Year in Review. If you lose one of your legacy app icons, you can retrieve it by re-reviewing your most recent Year in Review in the Transactions tab.
