# Creating Manual Internal Transfer Payments

**Source:** https://help.copilot.money/en/articles/4235839-creating-manual-internal-transfer-payments

If you are tracking a credit card or loan account manually, then you will need to create an internal transfer transaction when you make a payment to the manual account from a linked depository account (checking or savings).

*NOTE: To learn more about transaction types, please see this [article](https://intercom.help/copilotmoney/en/articles/3971267-transaction-types).*

For a payment from a linked account to a manual credit card or loan account, you will need two internal transfer transactions:
[![screenshot](../../screenshots/transactions/01-001.png)](https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/545209284/e2cd414390788010e667446a/001.png?expires=1773322200&signature=41f78988c8a95555e16d575d9e26c93a459aee185e6243229512ea866362de38&req=cSQiFMl3n4lbFb4f3HP0gB1vZRMgYwj8Zl5R0SN2VjFuFWF6cadl1MaZ6YwY%0A%2BZE3UihkEjf5TayPzw%3D%3D%0A)
An **Outgoing Internal Transfer**from your checking or savings account. If this account is linked in Copilot, then this transaction is automatically posted.

An **Incoming Internal Transfer**to your manual credit card account, which you need to create manually.

To create the **Incoming Internal Transfer** for your manual credit card account payment, please follow these steps:

From the Transactions view, select **Add a New Transaction**

Select **Transfer**
[![screenshot](../../screenshots/transactions/02-002.png)](https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/545209633/70c8dbfb2ad6c4e89174fd31/002.png?expires=1773322200&signature=09c628e8c4576996811674798ce82f30fbfaf82d3485a6e0f1cc9e9e55b6f700&req=cSQiFMl3m4JcFb4f3HP0gDDxYQud750Aa3gbLggm9fJJUiq57F0Zew1SRp5S%0Ap0DjLCOWvUWcOy1vSA%3D%3D%0A)
Update the date of your payment, if necessary, and **Continue**
[![screenshot](../../screenshots/transactions/03-003.png)](https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/545209889/b5520fa7d5e9017447d16c31/003.png?expires=1773322200&signature=b407a3bc5bea0b3f3e023681192c19cdaea9c4d0a70dbd09b9dfa70fe8dafbc7&req=cSQiFMl3lYlWFb4f3HP0gLR1kJFqUf62nlbePUf%2FxtwoYjMgfXzFT4iJNO0t%0A0MqK7SCOTHHwINsYCA%3D%3D%0A)
Add the **Transaction Name**
[![screenshot](../../screenshots/transactions/04-004.png)](https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/545210159/720823036b5429b149a729c6/004.png?expires=1773322200&signature=f5cb98e09b3a5e4cdc2da61f8adeee36d07b242ddcaddbf7c4ccbab1d5045cbe&req=cSQiFMh%2BnIRWFb4f3HP0gEu%2FJBL%2BOf5jW5HcMlCWgREzhpfB%2BxQpMi10%2Bnl2%0ANFXfHl%2BHIGHQUAyASQ%3D%3D%0A)
Enter the payment amount and select **Incoming**
[![screenshot](../../screenshots/transactions/05-005.png)](https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/545210427/06b4ed085c7bcf338ed2cb73/005.png?expires=1773322200&signature=c0023cc8c39b573f2eb74a1a9ac35acbe90a45fe4693f7b45946a360b8be31ff&req=cSQiFMh%2BmYNYFb4f3HP0gCfdbbFOcPzyvNu5zbpWb0J4N%2FPn2HwM6t7p1K3X%0AAP7TYiQzEgz51uNtGA%3D%3D%0A)
Tap on the account, scroll to your manual credit card, select it and **Save**
[![screenshot](../../screenshots/transactions/06-006.png)](https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/545210693/a6867757930cfa05bb5e4ffa/006.png?expires=1773322200&signature=a640b3214be479c933e9bdff5089ad7ad32ff58db74e642c5c8954a37c3455d2&req=cSQiFMh%2Bm4hcFb4f3HP0gCqCb7el%2BTfLuBozGoRIOigVopkGwcEbZV4chSdP%0AkTEngXBDau6bA%2FAZ7Q%3D%3D%0A)[![screenshot](../../screenshots/transactions/07-007.png)](https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/545210756/58adbcc3976612c4cabdf77a/007.png?expires=1773322200&signature=de38f5f7d462e24a3870c47005b0f70bfb56d5a5e482d60342e3ff5a2e41eed3&req=cSQiFMh%2BmoRZFb4f3HP0gAo3oLjPdQXD4q4uM6aUGPpTdlBjAtUNK48Za6vi%0A1wu%2Bn30BBvAa9NGM4w%3D%3D%0A)
After creating this Internal Transfer transaction, your payment will be reflected on your manual credit card account balance.
