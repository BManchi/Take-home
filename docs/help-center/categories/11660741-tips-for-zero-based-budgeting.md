# Tips for Zero-Based Budgeting

**Source:** https://help.copilot.money/en/articles/11660741-tips-for-zero-based-budgeting

Copilot Money offers flexibility in how you categorize transactions and [set up your budget](https://help.copilot.money/en/articles/11157550-quick-start-guide#h_f428faacf7). While Copilot isn't built specifically as a zero-based budgeting tool, here are our suggestions for maintaining this kind of budget in the Categories tab:

## **Step 1**

Set your total monthly budget to equal your total monthly income.

## **Step 2**

If you have any money left in your budget at the end of the month, move that amount of funds to a Savings and/or Investment account.
[![screenshot](../../screenshots/categories/03-image.png)](https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/gw2wbwl7/1602163738/36e0ea5f63a6e55c1c2d85587e52/image.png?expires=1773322200&signature=1bc9968a5615fa87132aa75c8bd40df6b6cc5174f7cc551c923bf9fd7f24cb35&req=dSYnFMh4noZcUfMW1HO4zVpiCj6n1Bq7dL31qJWFJhb%2F4nh%2BQKLZnNrj8S%2FM%0AxCMAOs5nKcCqq%2BIzhQs%3D%0A)[![screenshot](../../screenshots/categories/04-image.png)](https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/gw2wbwl7/1602166190/b886fd616f692ab6f876065abe10/image.png?expires=1773322200&signature=89cae29022b3cbeb9d55a7151070e8cb293ef58fe289d6fb35403be78a81caa3&req=dSYnFMh4m4BWWfMW1HO4zRNQf7ccs33N7tff%2BM%2FdpheXK%2FOhWjhHKGeKQ8yq%0A4KfYYJpBoke0v6TRY7k%3D%0A)
## **Step 3**

Make sure the outgoing *Internal Transfer*transaction(s) are marked as *Regular*, and categorize them as "Savings" or "Investments." Now you can see how every income dollar is accounted for in your budget.
