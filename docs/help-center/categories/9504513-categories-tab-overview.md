# **Categories Chart**

At the top of the Categories tab you'll see a comparison of the amount spent this month and your total budget for the month. If budgeting is not enabled, this will compare your spending to the amount spent in the previous month.

Below the chart will be your categories organized by spend amount.

# **Category Colors**

The bars for each category change depending on your spending rate for the current month. If the bar associated with a category is green, this means you're on pace to stay within your budget for that category. The bar will be yellow to orange if you are on pace to go over budget based on your spend so far this month. Once you are over budget, this bar will turn red. Outlined bars represent expected spend from [Recurrings](https://help.copilot.money/en/articles/3760068-create-recurrings).

When viewing previous months, spending bars will only be green or red to indicate you are either under or at budget, or if you're over budget.
[![screenshot](../../screenshots/categories/02-image.png)](https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/1170269952/ce2a324b3bd1112abe25a1e9/image.png?expires=1773322200&signature=1467195e634a2c2acf96af938d6bdf68d3542f46e35c153bd1df1957af147354&req=dSEgFst4lIhaW%2FMW1HO4zaepTxccgq4tCrACFNrxUPQz3FmBVmfJLOnOU0QN%0A6GNour%2B2HZWgmlxFFLk%3D%0A)
You can tap on the chart to view your total spend for the year and average monthly spend. You can view past years and months by tapping to the right of Yearly Metrics or Monthly Metrics. Learn more about how we calculate Key Metrics **[here](https://help.copilot.money/en/articles/6918427-understanding-key-metrics-for-spending)**.
