# Genmojis in Copilot

**Source:** https://help.copilot.money/en/articles/10269668-genmojis-in-copilot

With the launch of iOS 18.2, iPadOS 18.2, and macOS Sequoia 15.2, Apple has introduced a new type of emoji, **[the genmoji](https://www.apple.com/newsroom/2024/12/apple-intelligence-now-features-image-playground-genmoji-and-more/)**!

**Please note:**Genmojis are only available on iPhone 15 Pro and above.

---

Copilot supports generating custom genmojis for category and recurring emojis. Simply tap on the category or recurring emoji in the detail view to change the emoji. Then, enter a description in the emoji search to generate and select a genmoji.

To see your genmojis across all Copilot apps, you'll need to be on iOS 18.2, iPadOS 18.2, and macOS Sequoia 15.2 on your devices. If your device is on a previous version, you'll simply see a "?" in place of the genmoji.
