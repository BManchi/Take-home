# Tracking Your Car and Other Physical Assets

**Source:** https://help.copilot.money/en/articles/10760871-tracking-your-car-and-other-physical-assets

We do not currently support an auto valuation integration, but you can [create a manual Cash account](https://help.copilot.money/en/articles/4537532-creating-manual-accounts) for this asset with the current estimated value. Then you can update the balance as frequently as you'd like to track it in your Net Worth.

You can also use manual Cash accounts to track physical assets such as art, jewelry, and precious metals (like gold).

👋 Still have questions? Contact us via the in-app chat.
