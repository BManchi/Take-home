# Credit Utilization

**Source:** https://help.copilot.money/en/articles/10310069-credit-utilization

When viewing your credit card accounts in the Accounts tab, the dot colors next to each card’s utilization indicate the percentage of the credit limit you’ve used.

A lower utilization will show a green dot, while higher usage will cause the color to change from yellow to orange to red, reflecting increasing levels of utilization.

The green dot turns to yellow once you go over 33% utilization.
